About Us

Ink&You is a workshop of personalized books. We create stories based on your memories, emotions and ideas. Each book is a unique work that reflects your personality or the story of a loved one.

We use only premium materials and pay attention to every detail — from the structure of the text to the quality of the print. Our mission is to help you preserve the most valuable moments in the form of a beautiful book that will become a family heirloom. We work with you at every stage: we collect your experiences, develop the concept, write the text, select the design and print a book you will be proud of.

How it works

  1. Application

    You choose the format and submit an application.

  2. Questionnaire

    We send questions to collect your story.

  3. Concept

    We prepare a written concept for your book. You approve it or make adjustments (all formats).

  4. Writing

    We create the text of your book based on the approved concept.

  5. Edits

    (Only Masterpiece Edition) — after writing the final text you can make minor corrections.

  6. Design & layout

    We choose or create the cover and lay out the book.

  7. Printing & delivery

    We send the finished book with an eternal warranty.
