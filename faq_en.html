FAQ

How long does it take to create a book?

On average 10–12 business days from receiving answers to our questionnaire.

Can I add photos?

Yes. In Softcover — up to 5 photos, in Hardcover — up to 10 photos, in Masterpiece Edition — any number by agreement.

Can I change the text after writing?

Yes, only in the Masterpiece Edition.

Is there a warranty?

Yes, all books are backed by an eternal warranty.

Do you deliver outside Azerbaijan?

Yes, delivery is available according to courier rates.

How will my data be handled?

At Ink&You, your privacy is our top priority. Everything you share with us remains safe and protected. Your stories, ideas and personal data are processed with the utmost care and respect.

Disclaimer

We create literary works based on your answers in the questionnaire. We do not recreate real events word for word and are not witnesses to these events. We reserve the right to make artistic changes within the agreed concept. If there are no specific details in your answers (for example, dialogue lines), we create them based on the context.
